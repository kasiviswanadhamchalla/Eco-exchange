# IndustryConnect

## Problem Statement

Manufacturing industries generate waste materials such as:

- Steel Scrap
- Fly Ash
- Plastic Scrap
- Textile Waste
- Wood Waste
- Glass Waste

These materials are often discarded despite having market value.

At the same time:

- Recyclers
- Construction Companies
- Cement Manufacturers
- Packaging Industries

purchase similar materials as raw inputs.

IndustryConnect connects waste producers and waste consumers through a B2B marketplace.

---

# Goals

Allow organizations to:

1. List waste materials
2. Search materials
3. Place offers
4. Purchase inventory
5. Track transactions
6. Measure waste reused

---

# Functional Requirements

## Seller

- Register organization
- Create listing
- Upload images
- Upload quality certificates
- Manage inventory
- Accept offers
- Reject offers
- View sales history

## Buyer

- Search listings
- View material details
- Place offer
- Buy material
- Track orders
- Download certificates

## Logistics Partner

- View assigned shipments
- Accept shipment
- Update shipment status

## Admin

- Verify organizations
- Suspend organizations
- Moderate listings
- View platform analytics

---

# Non Functional Requirements

- High availability
- Event driven
- Distributed architecture
- Idempotent operations
- Audit logging
- Distributed tracing
- Search performance < 500ms
